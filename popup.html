In order for this to work be on the https://www.kroger.com/cl/coupons/ page.

Load all of the coupons onto the page by scrolling all the way down to the bottom of the page.

Click the "Click Coupons" button.

The script will click the coupons with a 1 second delay between each click.

Kroger only allows you to load 150 coupons onto your card

There is a log in the javascrpt console to tell you how many coupons it detected and when it completed.
